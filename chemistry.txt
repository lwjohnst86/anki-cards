What is hydrolysis?; A reaction involving breaking a bond using water (think NaCl, aka salt, dissolved in water).

What is lipolysis?; Breakdown of an esterified fatty acid (usually TAG) into free fatty acids using hydrolysis (the H2O breaks them apart).

What is esterification?; Combining a carboxylic acid and alcohol together (a -COOH with a -OH, respectively)

What is phosphorylation?; Transferring a phosphate group ([$]PO_4^{-3}[/$]) from a donor to a recipient molecule.

What is a carboxylic acid?; -COOH group at the end.

What is an alcohol?; -OH group at the end.
